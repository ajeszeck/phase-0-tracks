Random Animal Facts!

They are probably all true...

Hilarious Facts I found

  • Ostriches can run faster than horses, and the males can roar like lions.
  • A housefly hums in the key of F.
  • Most elephants weigh less than the tongue of a blue whale.

Mildly Terrifying Facts I found

  1. A tarantula spider can survive for more than two years without food.
  2. A python can swallow a rabbit whole and may eat as many as 150 mice in a six-month period.
  3. There is a shark called ‘goblin shark’ which has only been seen around 50 times since its discovery in 1897.
